PSN Travel Frenzy: Tourism Trivia Lead Generation

2019 – 2020 · Marketing Specialist

A travel startup with no existing customer base and no marketing infrastructure needed to build pipeline from scratch. I conceived and executed an end-to-end campaign, entirely offline, entirely from nothing.

Cold-approached tour operators and tourist destinations within a two-hour radius to secure sponsors willing to contribute prizes in exchange for social media mentions. Entry required an email address, converting foot traffic directly into a qualified lead list, which became the foundation for the company's first email marketing strategy.
